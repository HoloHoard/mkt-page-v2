Design a modern, dark-themed SaaS landing page for HoloHoard, a free TCG (trading card) collection tracker app. The design should feel energetic, collector-friendly, and tech-forward.

Brand & Visual Identity
Color Palette:

Primary background: #0D0D1A (very dark navy/almost black)
Secondary background: #12122A (dark navy, used for alternating sections)
Light section background: #EEF4FF (very light blue-white, used for the TCG logo carousel section)
Primary accent / CTA buttons: #FF1F8E (hot pink/magenta)
Secondary accent: #6B3BFF (bright purple)
Gradient hero: diagonal gradient from #7B2FFF (purple) to #3B7BFF (blue)
Heading highlight color: #00CFFF (cyan/aqua, used on key feature headings)
Text on dark: #FFFFFF (primary), #AAAACC (secondary/muted)
Text on light: #1A1A2E (dark navy)

Typography:

Font family: Inter (sans-serif) — or Be Vietnam Pro (used on site)
Hero headline: 64–72px, bold/black weight, white
Animated subheading (cycling words): 40px, cyan #00CFFF, bold
Section headings (H2): 40–48px, bold — alternates between white and hot pink #FF1F8E per section
Body text: 16–18px, regular weight, muted white #AAAACC
Feature bullet labels: 14–16px, semi-bold, white
CTA button text: 16px, bold, white

Logo & Nav:

Logo: Hexagonal shield icon (dark teal/navy with "H" letter) + "HoloHoard" wordmark in white, bold
Nav links (desktop): Features | FAQ | Ideas & Feedback | TCG Blog — white, no underline, spaced
Nav CTA button: "Start Collecting" — filled black pill button, white text, top right
Nav bar: transparent with slight blur/dark background when scrolled, sticky

UI Components & Style Details
Buttons:

Primary: Hot pink #FF1F8E, rounded pill (border-radius 50px), white bold text, subtle hover glow
Secondary/outline: White border, transparent fill, white text, pill shape
Nav CTA: Black fill, white text, pill shape

Feature Bullet Tags:

Dark navy/charcoal rounded pill (#1A1A2E or similar)
White check icon + white text inside
Slight border or glow effect

App Screenshots:

Shown in phone frame or frameless (borderless)
Dropped into gradient purple/blue bubble/blob containers
Some have dashed border frames (screenshot carousel section)
Overlapping/layered layout in hero

Decorative Elements:

Amoeba/blob shapes as image containers (organic, rounded forms)
White floating circles of various sizes as accent (database section)
Section backgrounds alternate between near-black and dark navy

Section Layout:

Max content width ~1200px, centered
Alternating 2-column layouts (text + screenshot, flipping sides each section)
Generous vertical padding (80–120px per section)
Mobile: stack to single column, full width screenshots


Tone
Energetic, collector-passion-driven, modern fintech/app aesthetic. Think "Robinhood meets trading card hobby." Dark mode first, with strategic hot pink and cyan accents for energy and calls to action.
